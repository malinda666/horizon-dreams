About Us
Initially a dance label in the late 90’s we felt the need to elevate technology in the music industry, through technology and high level partnerships we managed to simplify and optimize the concept of digital audio releasing. By the means of aggregation and distribution we’ve gathered a roster of 1M+ tracks and over 100 k albums distributed through our network. Through our diverse  collection of  brands we continue to push the envelope and develop backend solutions for our partners.
